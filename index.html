About JAK Generation

Main Business Of The Company Is To Direct Towards Industrial Solutions

JAK Generation is a fully owned Saudi company based in the Eastern part of Saudi Arabia. Jak is the industrial support service provider for petrochemical plants, oil and refineries, Dewatering services, steel plants, fertilizer plants, and the construction sector in Saudi Arabia.

We have grown steadily and won many accolades and appreciation. A commitment to the job. quality of work, seamlessness to provide total solutions, and desire to surpass client expectations helped in achieving the milestone, We grew from strength to strength by diversifying our business category right from equipment rental to construction and from trading to manpower solutions.
